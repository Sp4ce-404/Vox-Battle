GAMING VOICE INTEGRATION GUIDE

!
MOBILE AUDIO LIMITATION
Android 9+ security blocks apps from directly feeding audio into another app's microphone input. Read below to connect to Free Fire / PUBG!
01

SPEAKER LOOPBACK METHOD (EASY)

Turn on HEAR MYSELF, set phone volume high, and start playing. Your phone speaker will play your modified voice, and your headset or internal mic will pick it up and transmit it into the game lobby.

02

TWO-PHONE / AUX METHOD (PRO)

Play the game on one phone. Open this app on a second phone. Connect the headphone jack of the voice-changer phone to the microphone jack of the gaming phone using an AUX splitter. Your modified voice will feed directly into the game crystal clear!

03

PC EMULATOR METHOD

If playing Free Fire or PUBG on PC (via BlueStacks, LDPlayer, Gameloop, etc.), open this app in your PC browser, turn on HEAR MYSELF, and set your game input to "Stereo Mix" or your Virtual Audio Cable output.

FAQ

Will this lag?

We use low-latency Web Audio buffers. For the absolute lowest delay, use wired headphones instead of Bluetooth gaming headsets which add 150ms+ of Bluetooth latency.

Does this work offline?

Yes! Once installed on your phone, VoxBattle loads and runs entirely offline without internet, saving your mobile data while gaming.
