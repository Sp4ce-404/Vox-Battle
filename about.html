THE MANIFESTO

"Your voice is a weapon. Wield it."

VoxForge was born out of a need for absolute anonymity in competitive gaming. We believe your voice should be a tool, a weapon, and a mask — never a liability.

We are tired of being doxxed by a 13-year-old with a screen recorder. We are tired of high-pitched squeakers ending our ranked grind. We are tired of being us.

So we built the forge.

THE TECH

🔊

Web Audio API

Native browser audio graph. No installs, no plugins, no dependencies. Your soundcard does the work.

⚡

AudioWorklet

Off-main-thread DSP. Sample-accurate modulation at 48kHz. The same tech used in pro audio plugins.

🌐

PWA Architecture

Progressive Web App. Install once, run anywhere. Updates ship the moment you reconnect.

🧠

Procedural FX

1,200+ presets generated from synthesis graphs, not recordings. Infinite variations, zero storage cost.
